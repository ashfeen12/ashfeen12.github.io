About Me

I am a Marie Curie PhD researcher at Politecnico di Milano, nearing the completion of my doctoral studies in Environmental Engineering. My research focuses on the transport and removal of contaminants in porous media, employing both experimental and computational methods. I have a strong background in chemistry, with experience in molecular dynamics simulations, reactive transport modeling, and water quality analysis.

Research Interests

  • Contaminant transport and remediation
  • Water quality monitoring and modeling
  • Nanoparticle fate and transport
  • Molecular dynamics and DFT simulations
  • Environmental risk assessment
